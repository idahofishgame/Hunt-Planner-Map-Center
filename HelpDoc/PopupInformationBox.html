GETTING AROUND THE MAP: Popup Information Box


When you click on the map, a Popup Information Box appears and the attributes associated with the selected features are listed. Popup Information Box information will only appear for layers that are turned on.



In the top left-hand corner of the Popup Information Box shown above, you see '1 of 3'.  This means there is attribute information for 3 different layers.  if you click the arrow in the right-hand corner you will see the attribute information for the other two layers. Click 'Zoom to' in the bottom left-hand corner to zone to the whole feature identified in the Popup Information Box. To close the box click the 'X' in the upper right-hand corner.


                               


NOTE: When the Measurement Tools content panel is open, Popup Information Boxes are disabled.
