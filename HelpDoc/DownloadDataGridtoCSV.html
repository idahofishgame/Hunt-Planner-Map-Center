TOOLBAR: Download DataGrid to CSV


If you would like to save the attribute data in the DataGrid as a CSV*, activate this tool by clicking on the icon.


Depending on the browser you are using and the browser download settings, you will be prompted to download the CSV and/or open the CSV in Excel.


*CSV - Comma-separated values file.  It stores tabular data in plain-text form.  To save it in another format, open it in Excel and re-save.
