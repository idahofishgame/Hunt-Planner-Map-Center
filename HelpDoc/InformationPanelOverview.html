INFORMATION PANEL: Information Panel Overview


The Information Panel is what you call an Accordion Panel because it displays collapsible content panels for presenting information in a limited amount of space.  Click the headers to expand/collapse each content panel to: view the Legend, Turn Layers On/Off, Search For Properties, or use the Measurement Tools.


To see more of the map on your screen, you can collapse the Information Panel by clicking the small gray arrow in the upper right-hand corner of the Information panel or by dragging the splitter bar located on the right side of the Information Panel right/left to expand/contract it.
