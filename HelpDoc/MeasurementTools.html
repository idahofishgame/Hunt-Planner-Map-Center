INFORMATION PANEL: Measurement Tools


The measurement tools allow you to calculate area, distance, or get the longitude, latitude coordinate of a chosen point.  This handy widget also allows you to select the units of the results.



To active a measurement tool just click one of the icons. Once a tool is activated,  a units selection dropdown list will appear to the right of the icons.



To measure area, draw a polygon around your desired area to measure by clicking your mouse at each vertex.  When you are done drawing the polygon, double click the mouse on your last vertex.   The results will display under 'Measurement Results' in the content panel.



To measure distance, draw a line across your desired distance to measure by clicking your mouse at the start and subsequently creating vertices along the length of the distance you would like to measure by clicking the mouse.  Double click the mouse on your last vertex to get the results.



To get the coordinate of a location, simply click a point on the map.  A red flag will appear and the results will be displayed under 'Measurement Results' in the content panel.



NOTE: When the Measurement Tools content panel is open, Popup Information Boxes are disabled.
